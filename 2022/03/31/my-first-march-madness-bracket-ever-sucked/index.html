My first March Madness bracket ever... SUCKED!!!

2022-03-31

Well, when you’re at a young age like I am in college, you have to try some things you’ve never tried before to get that experience! No, it’s not what you think I’m talking about. I’m talking about making a NCAA March Madness Tournament Bracket. What did you think I was talk- you know what never mind, that’s besides the point.

Now, I’ll give out some warnings here. I have played sports since I was the young age of 4. I grew up playing baseball, football, soccer, and ice hockey. My only exception to the sport of basketball was that I played guard in the 8th grade because I was pretty tall for my age. So I technically played one season of basketball even though I only played probably a whopping 20 minutes all year, probably 8 games.

My knowledge of basketball is very close to about a rock when it comes to knowing data analytics and such in basketball because I just don’t follow or watch the sport that much. I was too busy playing hockey during the basketball season and I’ll be upfront, I am horrible at basketball. All I have for a skill set in basketball is that I could pass using my hockey ability and be able to run up and down the court because of my soccer, baseball, and hockey ability.

So, with my very little knowledge about basketball, I was told to take some stats and make a March Madness bracket out of who I thought had the best stats in certain areas. Want to hear my reasoning of why I chose what I did? ALL OFFENSE, NO DEFENSE! Now, the reason I say this is because I didn’t say I’ve never watch basketball before, I just have no interest in playing it or following it that much. If it is on at a sports bar, I’ll probably glance over at it here and there and one thing I notice the most, especially playing sports where defense is very crucial and important, is that players either very rarely play defense or not at all and I mean coming back to defend and actually pick up their man. So I decided to take all offensive stats of this past college basketball season and run RStudio based on that and hopefully pick my winning team from that!
